You What were NVIDIA's key risk factors related to export controls in their 2024 10-K?
AI

NVIDIA identified U.S. export control regulations as a significant risk, citing restrictions on A100 and H100 chip sales to China. The filing notes these restrictions created uncertainty around product substitution and could materially impact data center revenue — approximately 78% of total revenue in FY2024. NVIDIA also flagged risk of further tightening affecting future product generations.
